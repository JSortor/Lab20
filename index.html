Developing My Future

So, throughout my life there have been tons of people that asked me what i wanted to do with my future and like many young people my age these days I didnt really have a solid answer for them. For years I had been working in construction and thought I was on to a great career, but as the years past I grew restless and wanted something more out of life. After doing some thorough research I finally stumbled across coding. At first, these new concepts were completely foreign to me but after countless patient hours I grew a passion. What was the new passion?, I didn't know exactly but after more research I found Grand Circus and equipped with their answers to all my questions and a potential future career path with their help, I have never looked back. Now that I am enrolled and attending class everyday I see the future I want is right in front of me. Everyday presents a new challenge for me to continue growing and become a better developer than the day before. This is my favorite part and I cant wait to see what the future holds.
